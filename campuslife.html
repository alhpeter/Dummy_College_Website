Hostel Life — Safe, Simple, Supportive

Our on-campus hostel provides a secure, well-maintained home for students. Rooms are furnished, utilities are available 24/7, and the staff focus on comfort and discipline so students can concentrate on academics and campus life.

Facilities

  • Furnished rooms with attached bathrooms
  • 24×7 security, housekeeping and maintenance
  • Hot water, reliable electricity and Wi-Fi
  • Common room for study and recreation
  • Regular medical support and emergency procedures

Quick rules (short)

  • Ragging is strictly banned — zero tolerance.
  • Visitors must sign in at security; internal guests are restricted.
  • Quiet hours: 9:00 PM — 8:00 AM. Be considerate of roommates.
  • No unauthorized electrical appliances or cooking in rooms.
  • Keep rooms and common areas clean; periodic inspections happen.
Full rules & regulations (expand)

Attendance & conduct
All residents must follow the code of conduct expected at the Institute. Carry valid ID cards and respond to warden notices. Disciplinary action will follow repeated violations.

Visitors
All visitors including parents/guardians must register at hostel security. Unauthorized guests are not permitted in student rooms.

Room care
Students are responsible for furniture and fittings in allotted rooms. Do not remove or add furniture without written permission. Keep rooms clean; periodic inspections will be performed.

Prohibited items & activities
Hot plates, cooking, unauthorized heaters, loud music, gambling, smoking, alcohol, and narcotics are strictly prohibited. Screening pirated or unlicensed media is disallowed.

Safety & emergencies
For medical emergencies call the campus emergency number and inform the warden immediately. Conserve water and electricity — misuse may attract penalties.

Consequences
Violations will receive written warnings; up to three warnings may lead to eviction from the hostel. Serious offenses (including ragging) will be reported to legal authorities.

  1. Maintain decorum and respect other residents.
  2. Make absence notifications to the warden for vacations/holidays.
  3. Do not keep valuables unattended; hostel management is not responsible for losses.
  4. Follow any additional rules posted on the hostel notice board.
