About Gopalan College of Engineering and Management

An AICTE approved college affiliated to VTU, Gopalan College of Engineering and Management (GCEM) offers a state-of-the-art infrastructure, updated software and technology, modern workshops, seminar halls, auditoriums, sports amenities and well-stocked libraries. The institute focuses on creating an environment that encourages and provides for holistic development. We aim to ensure the students technical know-how and domain excellence which gives them an edge over.
