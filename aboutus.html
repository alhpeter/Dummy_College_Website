Who we are

Gopalan College of Engineering and Management (GCEM), Whitefield offers a vibrant learning environment in the heart of Bangalore's tech hub. With a future-ready curriculum, experienced faculty, and strong industry focus, GCEM helps students turn knowledge into real-world skills.
Backed by the legacy of the Gopalan Group, the campus blends academic excellence with an active student life, modern infrastructure, and top-notch sports and cultural opportunities. At GCEM, learning goes beyond classrooms, it's about growth, confidence, and building a successful future.

The GCEM campus offers a supportive and engaging environment designed to enhance both academic and personal growth. Modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, a well-stocked library, and dedicated research spaces provide students with the resources needed for effective learning and exploration. Beyond academics, the college encourages active student participation through cultural events, technical clubs, sports facilities, and extracurricular activities. These platforms help students build leadership skills, teamwork, and creativity, contributing to a well-rounded college experience. Located in Whitefield, Bangalore, GCEM also benefits from close proximity to major IT hubs and industries, enabling industry exposure, internships, and collaborative opportunities that bridge the gap between education and employment.
