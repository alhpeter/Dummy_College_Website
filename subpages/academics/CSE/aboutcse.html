About Computer Science and Engineering

This is one of the most prestigious courses offered by this institution with an average Intake for CSE Department in increased for academic year 2024 from 120 to 180. This course covers most of the advanced topics in computer hardware and software. This will enable the students to get a thorough idea of the theory and design of computer architecture, networks, communication etc.

The course provides facility to do experiments on software and computer hardware in order to gain a strong background of the engineering technologies in computer communication and network. The curriculum offers to develop the programming skills of the students from the very basic level to a professional level to the emerging challenges of the present software development in industries. Projects are also included in the final semesters to get a hand on experience with highly skilled professionals of the industry.
