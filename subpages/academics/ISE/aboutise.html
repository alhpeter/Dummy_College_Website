About Information Science and Engineering

Established in 2023, the Department of Information Science & Engineering (ISE) is one of the newest and most dynamic departments at GCEM. The department focuses on creating a balanced academic environment that blends strong technical foundations with opportunities for creativity, collaboration, and real-world exposure.

ISE encourages students to go beyond textbooks through a wide range of technical and non-technical activities, including hackathons, workshops, cultural programs, and inter-departmental events. These platforms allow students with diverse interests and skill sets to work together, exchange ideas, and transform innovative thoughts into meaningful outcomes.

A key driving force behind student engagement in the department is the ARISE Club, which actively organizes and supports both technical and non-technical events. The club serves as a space for students to develop leadership skills, explore new interests, and participate in activities that enhance both professional and personal growth.

With a focus on adaptability, teamwork, and continuous learning, the ISE department aims to prepare students to meet the evolving demands of the technology industry while fostering a vibrant and inclusive student community.
